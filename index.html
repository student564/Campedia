Camp starts here.

Created in 2024, Campedia is an under-construction website made to serve as a dedicated encylopedia for new campers looking either for a place to start or to gain more experience to improve their overall time in the great outdoors. Having always been fascinated with nature and overjoyed with the end of COVID-19 lockdown, I decided to express my newfound freedom by way of taking a camping trip out at Griffin City Park. Having never been camping before, I had quite the rough time trying to adjust and decided to use my coding skills to make sure no one else had to go through the same pains. Take a click of any link to start.


Each link will take you to a page that will detail the aspect of camping it pictures. The linked pages will be divided into three sections: What To Look For When Purchasing, Common Mistakes, and Proper Use. The navigation menu will take you to an About_Us, Contact_Us, and Events page. The Events page will showcase various activities on the site. The Contact_Us page will allow the user to send us their contact informtion. Finally, the About_Us page will show a small personal Resume.
